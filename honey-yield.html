Estimating Your Honey Harvest

Honey yield varies by frame size, how full the frames are, and how well-capped the honey is. A full deep frame typically holds 6–7 pounds of honey.

To estimate your harvest: count full frames, multiply by the per-frame yield, and adjust for average fullness. Leave enough honey for the bees — at least 60–80 lbs for overwintering in northern climates.
