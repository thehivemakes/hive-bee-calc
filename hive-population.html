How to Estimate Hive Population

Colony population assessment helps beekeepers make management decisions: when to add supers, when to split, when to combine weak colonies, and when to treat for diseases.

A standard deep Langstroth frame fully covered on both sides holds approximately 5,000 adult bees. A full frame of capped brood contains about 6,000 cells that will emerge as adult bees within 12 days.
