About BeeCalc

BeeCalc is the first dedicated suite of free beekeeping calculators on the web. Manage varroa mite loads, estimate hive populations, plan winter feeding, calculate honey yields, and time your nectar flow — all from your phone in the apiary.

Every tool runs in your browser with no data leaving your device. Share results via URL — send your varroa count to your bee club or bookmark your feeding schedule.
